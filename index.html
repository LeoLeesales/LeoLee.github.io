不锈钢板保护膜Stainless Steel Plate Protective Film

专为不锈钢板材表面保护设计Specially designed for surface protection of stainless steel plates

不锈钢板保护膜是我公司针对不锈钢板材研发的专用保护产品，采用高品质PE原料，配合特殊的压敏胶配方，具有优异的防刮、防氧化、防污染性能。膜面平整，粘性适中，易撕不残胶，不影响不锈钢板材的表面光洁度和后续加工。

Stainless steel plate protective film is a special protective product developed by our company for stainless steel plates. It uses high-quality PE raw materials and special pressure-sensitive adhesive formula, with excellent scratch resistance, oxidation resistance and pollution resistance. The film surface is flat, the viscosity is moderate, easy to tear without residue, and does not affect the surface finish and subsequent processing of stainless steel plates.
