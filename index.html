青岛容顺新材料有限公司Qingdao Rongshun New Materials Co.,Ltd

青岛容顺新材料有限公司是一家专业从事PE保护膜研发、生产、销售的高新技术企业，坐落于美丽的海滨城市青岛。公司拥有先进的生产设备和专业的技术团队，年产能达万吨级，产品广泛应用于不锈钢板、uPVC型材、窗户玻璃、地毯等多个领域。

Qingdao Rongshun New Materials Co.,Ltd is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production and sales of PE protective film, located in the beautiful coastal city of Qingdao. The company has advanced production equipment and professional technical team, with an annual output of 10,000 tons, and its products are widely used in stainless steel plates, uPVC profiles, window glass, carpets and other fields.
