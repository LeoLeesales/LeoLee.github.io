Qingdao Rongshun New Materials Co.,Ltd is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, production and sales of PE protective film, located in the beautiful coastal city of Qingdao. The company has advanced production equipment and professional technical team, with an annual output of 10,000 tons, and its products are widely used in stainless steel plates, uPVC profiles, window glass, carpets and other fields.

We adhere to the business philosophy of "quality first, customer foremost". All products pass strict quality inspection to ensure stable and reliable protective film products for customers. 不锈钢板保护膜 Stainless Steel Plate Film

专为不锈钢板材设计，具有优异的防刮、防氧化性能，易撕不残胶，不影响板材表面光洁度。支持高/中/低粘度定制，适应不同厚度板材的保护需求。

Designed for stainless steel plates, with excellent scratch and oxidation resistance, easy to tear without residue, does not affect the surface finish of the plate. Support high/medium/low viscosity customization to meet the protection needs of different thickness plates.

防刮伤 Scratch Resistant 无残胶 No Residue 定制粘度 Custom Viscosity

uPVC型材保护膜 uPVC Profile Film

适用于门窗uPVC型材的表面保护，耐候性强，适应户外存储和运输环境，有效防止型材表面划伤、污染。可印刷客户LOGO，提升产品品牌形象。

Suitable for surface protection of door and window uPVC profiles, with strong weather resistance, adapt to outdoor storage and transportation environment, effectively prevent scratch and pollution on the surface of profiles. Can print customer LOGO to enhance product brand image.

耐候性强 Weather Resistant 防污染 Pollution Proof 可印刷 Printable

窗户玻璃保护膜 Window Glass Film

高透性玻璃保护膜，施工过程中有效保护玻璃表面不被划伤、污染，移除无残留，操作简单。适用于建筑玻璃、家居玻璃等多种玻璃制品的临时保护。

High transparency glass protective film, effectively protect the glass surface from scratch and pollution during construction, remove without residue, easy to operate. Suitable for temporary protection of various glass products such as architectural glass and household glass.

高透光 High Transparency 易移除 Easy Removal 施工保护 Construction Protection

地毯保护膜 Carpet Protection Film

装修专用地毯保护膜，防滑易贴，防水防污，有效保护地毯在装修过程中不被损坏、污染。轻便易铺设，成本低，一次性使用更卫生。

Special carpet protective film for decoration, anti-slip and easy to stick, waterproof and stain proof, effectively protect the carpet from damage and pollution during decoration. Lightweight and easy to lay, low cost, more hygienic for one-time use.

防滑 Anti-slip 防水 Waterproof 装修专用 Decoration Special

定制化PE保护膜 Custom PE Protective Film

根据客户需求定制专属PE保护膜，可调整宽度（10mm-2000mm）、长度、厚度，颜色可选透明、蓝色、黑色等，支持印刷LOGO、文字、二维码等。

Customize exclusive PE protective film according to customer needs, can adjust width (10mm-2000mm), length, thickness, color options include transparent, blue, black, etc., support printing LOGO, text, QR code, etc.

尺寸定制 Size Custom 颜色定制 Color Custom LOGO印刷 LOGO Printing
